Privacy Policy for AI Dairy Farm Manager
Last updated: April 15, 2026

Thank you for using AI Dairy Farm Manager. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read it carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide Directly
Account information – name, email address, farm name, and password (stored securely).

Herd data – animal IDs, breed, birth dates, health events, treatments, milk production records, feeding schedules, and any notes you enter.

Media – photos or videos you capture via the camera or upload from your gallery (e.g., cow body condition, injury, or ear tag).

Location – if you grant location permission, we may collect your farm’s approximate or precise GPS coordinates to provide weather‑based recommendations or record grazing areas.

Communications – any messages you send to our support team.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically
Device information – model, operating system version, unique device identifiers.

App usage data – features used, time spent, crash logs, and performance data (through Firebase or similar analytics tools).

Notification tokens – to send you reminders (e.g., “Time to milk”, “Vaccination due”).

1.3 Camera & Gallery Access
Camera – used only when you actively take a photo of an animal, ear tag, or farm area. The App does not access your camera in the background.

Gallery – accessed only when you explicitly choose an existing photo/video from your device to attach to an animal’s record.

No facial recognition – we never attempt to identify humans from images.

1.4 Location Access (if enabled)
Background location – not used. Location is only collected when the App is open and you perform a location‑related action (e.g., marking a pasture boundary).

Purpose – linking weather data to your herd’s location, or mapping animal movements.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

Provide and maintain the App’s core features (herd management, milk tracking, health alerts).

Process AI analysis of uploaded images (e.g., detect an ear tag number or assess body condition score).

Send you push notifications for feeding, milking, vet appointments, or health alerts (you can disable these in settings).

Improve the App’s performance and fix bugs.

Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your farm data, animal records, or personal information to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Information
We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

With your consent – e.g., you export a health report to share with your veterinarian.

Service providers – cloud hosting (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud), analytics (Firebase), crash reporting (Sentry). They are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for the services we request.

Legal requirements – if required by law, court order, or government regulation (e.g., animal disease outbreak reporting).

Business transfers – if we are acquired or merged, your data will be transferred under the same privacy commitments.

4. Data Storage & Security
Storage location – Your data is stored on secure servers in [choose one: United States / European Union / your region].

Retention – We keep your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App. You may delete your account at any time; all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Security measures – Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3), encrypted backups, role‑based access controls, and regular security audits. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means.

5. Your Rights & Choices
Depending on your location (e.g., EU, UK, Brazil, California), you may have the right to:

Access – request a copy of your personal data.

Correct – update inaccurate information.

Delete – delete your account and all associated data (see “Account Deletion” below).

Export – receive a machine‑readable export of your herd data.

Opt out of marketing – we do not send marketing emails; only transactional/service notifications.

To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@aidairyfarm.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Account Deletion
You can delete your account directly inside the App:
Settings → Account → Delete Account
All herd records, images, and personal information will be permanently erased.

6. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us, and we will delete it.

7. International Data Transfers
If you use the App from outside our server’s region, your data may be transferred to and processed in that region. By using the App, you consent to such transfer.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the App (e.g., a pop‑up notice) or by email. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: privacy@aidairyfarm.com
